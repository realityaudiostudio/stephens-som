Our Story

A School Built on Passion

At Stephen's School of Music, we believe everyone has rhythm inside them. Located in the heart of Nilambur, our academy was founded to create a supportive, active environment where students don't just learn notes—they learn how to play together.

Our classrooms are always buzzing with creativity. From one-on-one mentorship to group jam sessions, we ensure no student ever feels left behind on their musical path.

Courses We Offer

Step into our classrooms. We offer practical, engaging lessons for all skill levels.

Guitar

Learn chord progressions, strumming patterns, and fretboard theory in a fun, interactive setting.

Keyboard

Develop hand independence and learn to read sheet music with our dedicated keyboard instructors.

Violin

Master bowing techniques, intonation, and classical to contemporary musical styles.

Drums

Build rhythm, coordination, and groove on a full acoustic drum kit with expert guidance.

Vocal

Find your voice. We teach breathing techniques, pitch control, and vocal health for singers.
